Section 03
Feature Importance
Gradient Boosting impurity-based importances across all 11 features. Top 3 highlighted in amber — they account for 64% of total importance.
⚠ Potential data artifact: days_rest_diff (rank #1, importance 0.28)

This feature ranks first despite being a simple fatigue proxy. The likely cause: players appearing in the dataset for the first time receive a default fill value of 14 days rest (no prior match found). If first-appearances are systematically skewed toward winners or losers — for example, because talented newcomers win their debut matches more often — the model may be capturing a dataset artefact rather than genuine rest-day signal.

Recommended fix: add a career_matches_to_date feature so the model can discount early-career observations, and re-evaluate whether days_rest_diff retains high importance on players with ≥ 20 career matches on record.
